Children's Scholarship Fund Baltimore

Children’s Scholarship Fund and its partner programs have provide scholarship since 1998, changing the lives of almost 166,000 children across the United States. CSF has funded scholarships worth $741 million across the country. CSF Scholars are more likely to graduate than their public school peers, as demonstrated by studies in the San Francisco Bay Area, Charlotte, Denver, and Philadelphia. Studies of CSF Scholars in Baltimore, Charlotte, Los Angeles, Memphis and Philadelphia found their test scores were higher than those of their counterparts in nearby public schools.

Family League of Baltimore

Family League of Baltimore has mobilized resources, empowered communities, and built the capacity of organizations to provide children and families with high quality programs and services that help them reach their full potential. Family League funds over 90 community‐based organizations to do this work and utilizes data to effectively measure these outcomes. In 2017, Family League granted $16,925,000 to organizations to help fulfill its mission of creating lasting outcomes for children and youth.
